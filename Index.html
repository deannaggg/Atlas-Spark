05 — Conversion Architecture

THE SALES
FUNNEL

Seven stages. Each one sovereign. Each designed to filter for the builder who is ready.

01
Awareness
THE SPARK HOOK
Paid social (Instagram Reels, YouTube pre-roll) and organic content amplifying the brand manifesto. Lead magnet: "The Sovereign Builder Blueprint" — a free download that delivers immediate value and filters for serious builders.
Channels: Instagram · YouTube · LinkedIn · Podcast
02
Capture
THE LANDING PAGE
Email opt-in for free webinar registration. Minimal form: Name + Email + Niche. Urgency mechanism: "Seats are sovereign and scarce — 47 remaining." Obsidian/violet design that signals premium before a word is read.
Conversion target: 35%+ opt-in rate
03
Warm
THE EMAIL SEQUENCE
5-email pre-webinar sequence. Email 1: Manifesto + origin story. Email 2: Social proof — member results. Email 3: The land opportunity teaser. Email 4: AI leverage case study. Email 5: Final urgency + reminder. Tone: declarative, authoritative, zero hype.
Sequence: 5 emails · 7-day window
04
Convert
THE LIVE WEBINAR
90-minute live event. Act 1 (20 min): The Problem — legacy systems are fracturing. Act 2 (40 min): The Solution — Atlas infrastructure + Spark AI leverage. Act 3 (30 min): The Offer — Catalyst tier revealed with founding member rate. Live Q&A closes with apply CTA.
Pitch window: Final 20 minutes · Replay 48 hours
05
Upsell
THE LAND BRIDGE
Post-webinar: members who apply within 48 hours receive exclusive access to the Atlas Land Deal deck. This bridges the membership sale with the land investment offer — two revenue streams, one funnel. Sovereign tier+ unlocks deal co-investment.
Revenue bridge: Membership → Land investment
06
Retain
THE ONBOARDING RITUAL
Welcome sequence: personalized video from founder. Access to the member portal. Calendar invite for first Spark event. Monthly Sovereign Frequency newsletter. Community integration via private workspace. The collective becomes the product.
Churn target: <5% monthly
07
Amplify
THE REFERRAL ENGINE
Sovereign members who refer a founding member receive one complimentary Spark event seat. Apex members receive a credit toward land deal allocation. The collective grows itself. Power begets power.
Mechanism: Credit-based referral program
